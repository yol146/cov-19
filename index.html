What is a coronavirus ?

2019 novel coronavirus (2019-ncov), was identified due to the case of viral pneumonia in wuhan in 2019, and was named by the world health organization on January 12, 2020. Coronavirus is a large family of viruses known to cause colds and more serious illnesses such as MERS and SARS. The novel coronavirus is a new strain of coronavirus that has never been found in humans before.
    Since December 2019, influenza and related diseases have been continuously monitored in wuhan, hubei province, and a number of cases of viral pneumonia have been detected, all of which have been diagnosed as viral pneumonia/lung infection. （The information is from https://baike.baidu.com/）
